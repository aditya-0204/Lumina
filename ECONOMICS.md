# ECONOMICS - Unit Economics & Revenue Model

## Converted Lead Value to Credex

**Assumption:** A "lead" = An engineering manager who filled the audit, saw >$500/month savings potential, and booked a Credex consultation call.

**Lead-to-customer journey:**
1. Audit shows $500/mo savings opportunity
2. Credex team reaches out → books 30-min consultation
3. ~30% close rate → customer purchases credits
4. Average credit purchase: $2,000-$5,000 one-time + renewals

**Conservative LTV calculation:**
- Avg one-time purchase: $3,000
- 70% of those renew annually at 50% of initial: +$1,050/year
- 3-year LTV: $3,000 + $1,050 + $1,050 = **$5,100 per converted lead**

(Upside: 40-50% of customers expand, could add $2-3k more)

## Conversion Funnel: Audit → Consultation → Sale

**Assumptions:**
- 100 audits/week by week 2
- 30% complete form (email captured): 30 leads
- 15% of leads book consultation (engaged enough): 4.5 consultations
- 30% of consultations close (already pre-qualified by savings amount): 1.35 customers/week

**Math:**
- 100 audits → 30 leads → 4.5 consultations → 1.35 customers
- Customer value: $5,100 LTV × 1.35 = **$6,885/week revenue**
- Cost: ~$0 (if organic only)
- **Payback:** Immediate (positive cash flow week 2)

|--------|----------------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|-----|-----|
| Month 1 (May) | 200 | 20% | 12 | 3.6 | $18,360 | $220k |

**What has to be true for $1M ARR in 18 months:**
1. ✓ Free acquisition channels remain accessible (not saturated)
2. ✓ Tool gets 5k+ audit completions by month 6
3. ✓ 25%+ email capture rate (current assumption: realistic)
4. ✓ 30%+ consultation-to-close rate (depends on Credex's sales team)
5. ✓ No major competitor launches (market risk)

**Upside lever:** If we add benchmark mode ("your spend/dev = $X, industry avg = $Y"), we unlock:
- Retention (users check monthly)
- Competitive positioning
- Upsell to analytics dashboard

**Break-even point:** Month 2 (if conversion rates hold)

## Revenue Model Options (Beyond Lead Generation)

- **Option 1** (Current): 100% leads to Credex consultations
- **Option 2**: Take 30% commission on credits purchased via our referral link
- **Option 3**: Premium version with PDF export, benchmarking ($5/user/month)
- **Option 4**: White-label to other credit providers (Lepton, Lambda, etc.)

**Recommendation:** Stick with Option 1 (lead generation) for Round 1. It's clean, measurable, and aligns with Credex's sales process.
