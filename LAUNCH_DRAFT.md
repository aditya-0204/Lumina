## Bonus: Launch Post Draft

Lumina helps startup teams figure out whether they are overpaying for AI tools. Instead of giving generic optimization advice, it asks what your team actually uses, what plans you are on, how many seats you have, and what kind of work you do. Then it shows where the real savings are, what you should keep, and what is worth changing.

The first version focuses on speed and clarity. You can go from input to audit in a few minutes, save the result, share it, and print it as a PDF brief for internal review. The long-term goal is to make AI software spend more understandable for teams that do not want to build a manual spreadsheet every time pricing changes.
