## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

**Q1: How do you know the best plan for my team?**  
A: We compare your current spend against all plans for each tool, your team size, and your use case. We recommend the cheapest plan that still fits your needs, not the most expensive. We also flag when a completely different tool would be cheaper.

**Q2: Do you make money off this tool?**  
A: Yes. Credex sells discounted AI credits. When the tool points someone toward a credit marketplace, Credex may earn a margin. That should be disclosed clearly so users can judge the recommendation in context.

**Q3: Do I have to book a call with Credex?**  
A: No. The audit should be useful on its own. If the savings are meaningful, a follow-up conversation is optional.

**Q4: What if I upload my actual invoice?**  
A: Right now users enter estimates manually. Invoice-assisted input is a natural next step because it would make the audit more accurate and reduce effort.

**Q5: Can I export the results?**  
A: Users can already create a shareable audit link. Export options like PDF can be added later if teams want a more formal report.
