## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

**Q1: How do you know the best plan for my team?**  
A: We compare your current spend against all plans for each tool, your team size, and your use case. We recommend the cheapest plan that still fits your needs—not the most expensive. We also flag when a completely different tool (Cursor vs. ChatGPT) would be cheaper.

**Q2: Do you make money off this tool?**  
A: Yes. Credex (the company behind this) sells discounted AI credits. When we recommend buying credits through Credex, they earn a margin. We disclose this upfront because transparent recommendations are more trustworthy than hidden ones.

**Q3: Do I have to book a call with Credex?**  
A: No. You get the full audit for free. If your savings are >$500/month, we invite you to chat with our team about maximizing your benefits. But you can ignore that and just use the recommendations.

**Q4: What if I upload my actual invoice?**  
A: Right now you input estimated spend. We're working on invoice upload so you can paste in your Stripe bill and we parse the actual numbers. Coming soon.

**Q5: Can I export the results?**  
A: Yes—you get a shareable URL to send to your team, and we're adding PDF export this month. You can also take a screenshot (it looks good).

## Why This Copy Works

1. **Honesty on economics** (Q2): Builds trust by being transparent about conflicts of interest
2. **No-pressure offer** (Q3): Removes friction ("you don't have to buy anything")
3. **Specific benefit** (copy): Numbers, not buzzwords
4. **Social proof**: Real quotes, not fabricated testimonials
5. **CTA clarity**: One clear action, not multiple options
